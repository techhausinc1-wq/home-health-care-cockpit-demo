Why the caregiver experience matters

Home health turnover runs 60% annually. Every replacement costs ~$2,500 in recruiting + training. Software that respects the caregiver's day is the cheapest retention lever available — cheaper than raises. The features below are the ones caregivers tell us actually move the needle.

Built into the caregiver app

▦
Today's schedule with drive guidance
Visits in time order. Tap the next visit → opens Apple/Google Maps with one tap. No copy-pasting addresses.
●
One-tap EVV check-in
GPS auto-verified at the patient's location. No separate state EVV app to fight with.
✉
In-app messaging
Direct line to RN supervisor and office scheduling. Caregivers' real questions get real answers without playing phone tag.
$
Live pay tracking
Hours-this-week and estimated pay update after every visit. The #1 retention question: "did I get paid for my hours?" — answered before they ask.
▲
My licenses + expiry alerts
Caregiver sees their own certifications and gets push reminders 60 / 30 / 7 days before expiry. Office stops chasing.
→
Time-off requests + shift swap
Request PTO, see open shifts you can pick up, swap with another caregiver — all without calling the office.

Engine note · cross-vertical

This same caregiver-on-the-phone pattern powers the HVAC tech app, plumbing tech app, pool tech app, pet sitter app, landscape crew app — every field service vertical in the catalog. Different industry vocabulary, same architecture: schedule + GPS verify + message + pay + license expiry. Build it once, ship it to 10+ products.
